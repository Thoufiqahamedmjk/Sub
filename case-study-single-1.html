Case Study: Traffic Growth from 1,200 to 38,000 Monthly Visitors

Updated March 2026 • Industry: Content Website • Duration: 6 Months

Client Background

A small niche website approached us with just 1,200 monthly visitors. No keyword planning, no optimization, and inconsistent content quality.

Challenges Identified

  • Lack of keyword strategy
  • Weak EEAT signals
  • Thin content (avg 650 words)
  • Zero internal linking structure
  • No schema markup

RankIQ Strategy Implemented

1. AI Keyword Library Selection

We selected 42 low-competition keywords using RankIQ’s pre-curated library and built content clusters around 5 pillar topics.

2. Full Content Optimization

Every article was passed through RankIQ’s Content Optimizer, improving:

  • Readability
  • Semantic keyword coverage
  • Content depth

3. Internal Linking Fix

We built a hub-and-spoke model linking all related articles, boosting topical authority.

Results After 6 Months

  • Organic Traffic: 1,200 → 38,000 (+3067%)
  • Top 10 Keywords: 3 → 47
  • Avg Ranking Time: 10–35 days
  • Bounce Rate Improvement: 71% → 56%

Proof of Result

Data cross-verified via:

  • Google Search Console
  • Google Analytics 4

External Supporting Research

To improve EEAT, references were taken from trustworthy sources:

  • Google Helpful Content Guidelines
  • Ahrefs Keyword Research Study
  • Moz Internal Linking Guide
